FAQ

The most frequently questions to help you understand better your needs

  • How much time does it take to implement your solutions?

    Giving an exact estimate to this question is very complicated, as it depends on multiple factors. In the case of wanting to digitize management processes, the variables that most influences are the size of the company, the number of employees, and the degree of scalability and maintainability that you want to implement. On the other hand, in the case of wanting to automate the quality assurance of the software, it will depend directly on the solution that is chosen among those that exist in the market, in addition to the number of test cases that are to be included and the integrations that are want to do.

  • What happen when my solution is finished and implemented?

    Once we have finished, the solution will be ready to be used in the day to day of your business without depending on us. For this, we offer training plans for your employees, in this way, you can maintain and update your solution without depending on a company outside your organization.

  • What happen if my solution stop working?

    You can contact us whenever you need and we will analyze the case immediately. All the errors that derive from the implementation of the tool will be corrected for free.

  • Mi solucion esta lista, pero prefiero que seais vosotros quienes esten a cargo de la herramienta

    No hay problema, desde MYQAD ofrecemos bolsas de horas mensuales y anuales que podras utilizar cuando desees, ya sea para añadir nuevas funcionalidades a tu ya existente solucion,optimizar y automatizar procesos o arreglar errores que no deriven de la implementacion de la solucion.
